Programme Overview

Udaan is a mentor-led preparation programme that adapts to your chosen exam – UPSC, SSC, Banking, CAT, State PSCs, and more. Every student receives a personalized roadmap based on strengths, weaknesses, timeline, and exam stage.

  • One-to-one sessions focused on clarity and execution
  • Personalized study plans with diagnostics and trackers
  • Continuous evaluation via MCQs, descriptive answers, essays
  • Motivational check-ins and structured accountability
